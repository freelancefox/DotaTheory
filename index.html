About

Originally started as the personal site of @freelancedota, DotaTheory is a fan site for Dota 2 founded in 2015. While this project has been put on hold numerous times over the years, we've been steadily making progress towards the goal of becoming the top source for high-level theorycrafting for Dota 2, and eventually hope to expand to other competitive esport titles.

Purpose

While fostering a community of like-minded theorycrafters, DotaTheory aims to revive the spirit of the old PlayDota site and bring that same level of passion to both a wider audience and a new era of gamers.

We aim to create the model fansite for the most hardcore of competitive gamers: by bringing curated guides, high-level theorycrafting, educational contest, fanart submissions, news and high-quality discussion together all under one roof.

Who are we?

The project is currently run solely by @FreelanceDota but we are absolutely looking to grow! From moderators to curators and content creators, if you're passionate about Dota 2 or web development, please reach out! Check out our Discord and Reddit for opportunities.
